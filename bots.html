Python RPG Game


This week, we used Python to make a minimalistic turn-based RPG, where you battle our Mentors In Training. I am working on taking the framework from this game and turning it into a full pokemon engine.
Progress so far has been slow, but I have all of next week to improve it. Instead of you and the enemy MIT's stats being pre-set and static, both the player and enemy pokemon are chosen from an array, with individual stats for health, attack, defence, special attack, special defence, and speed. Speed and the special stats aren't used yet, but that shouldn't be too complicated. Attack and defence are used in a equation based on the actual Pokemon games to calculate damage done.
Expect support for multiple moves, items, and using a team of pokemon!

...also you can't lose yet. That should be fixed soon.
