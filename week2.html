This week, I've made some major changes to my pokemon engine.
You and your enemy both have a team of six pokemon, and each pokemon has two unique moves! The game has functional speed stats, a physical/special split, critical hits, and pokemon switching! I plan to add usable items and type effectiveness soon.

This is easily the biggest project in terms of pure code. Over 300 lines of Python!

This week, AJ and I were fortunate enough to be able to develop for the HTC Vive. Our project is called Cube Warfare 2.
The original Cube Warfare was a game made in Unity by two of our mentors, where you fought an endless enslaught of murderous boxes. in Cube Warfare 2, you can up close and personal with guns, swords, shields, axes, and golf clubs. AJ worked on the programming for the game, I play tested and made assets.
